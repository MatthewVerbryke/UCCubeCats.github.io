CATiSE Program

Our high-altitude balloon program, also known as “CATiSE”, is fast paced and geared towards incoming freshman members. This year long program teaches the basics of systems engineering and allows them to gain experiences and skills before joining the CubeSat program. This program begins with brainstorming mission ideas for high-altitude balloons and then over the following months they design, test, and build every aspect of the system.
